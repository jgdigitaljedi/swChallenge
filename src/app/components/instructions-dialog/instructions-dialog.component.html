Instructions

Add a Person

Fill in person's name, age, and weight. Optionally fill in the person's friends. There will not be friends availible in the multi-select until at least 1 person has been added. Click 'Save Person' button.

Understanding the Visualization

The size of the cricle is directly related to the person's weight. The color of the circle indicates the person's age range and the legend can be referenced for determining their range. The links between circles show the 'friends' relationship. You can drag the circles around for some fun animations!
